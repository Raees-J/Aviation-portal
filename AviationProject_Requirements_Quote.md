# Aviation Project – Client Requirements & Quotation

- Optional: Licenses for premium tools (email, analytics, UI kits) if needed.

- Yes, ongoing costs for hosting, database, domain, email, and support. See monthly breakdown below.

## 8. Post-Year Maintenance/Updates

## 9. Servers

## 11. Recommended Storage Size
